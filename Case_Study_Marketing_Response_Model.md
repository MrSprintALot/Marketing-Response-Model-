## Business Problem
Marketing aims to improve campaign ROI by identifying customers most likely to accept promotional offers.

## Objective
Build a logistic regression model to predict which customers will respond to the next campaign.

## Methodology
1. Data preprocessing (encoding, scaling, tenure calculation)
2. Model training (Logistic Regression)
3. Evaluation (ROC, AUC, Accuracy, Precision, Recall)
4. Feature importance visualization

## Results
AUC: 0.895 | Accuracy: 0.888 | Precision: 0.707 | Recall: 0.433

## Key Insights
- Previous campaign acceptance strongly predicts future responses.
- Recency and web/store engagement are key behavioral indicators.
